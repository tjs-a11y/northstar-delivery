Questions
Common Questions
Do I need to be technical to use this? +
Not at all. We handle all the technical setup — white-labeling, configuration, and deployment. You get login credentials and a walkthrough. No code required.
I already have dispatch software. Can I just add specific modules? +
Yes — this is our overlay model. You can license individual modules that sit on top of your existing system. Most companies start with just the booking website or Grabber, then add more over time. We integrate, we don't replace.
How long does setup take? +
48 hours from license approval to your system going live. We configure everything — your logo, colors, domain, and any custom settings — and walk you through the platform on a call.
What does the revenue share mean exactly? +
At the end of each month you report your gross revenue and pay the agreed percentage on top of your flat fee. It only applies when you're generating revenue — we grow when you grow. It's the same model used by Shopify, Stripe, and most SaaS platforms.
Is the system actually running a live business? +
Yes. NorthStar Delivery Service at northstardeliveryservice.com runs on this exact platform with real customers, real drivers, and real payments. You can visit it right now. What you license is what you see.
What happens if something breaks? +
The platform runs on enterprise-grade infrastructure (Supabase, Stripe, GitHub Pages) that is maintained by those companies independently. For anything platform-specific, Growth and above tiers include priority support with a guaranteed response time.
